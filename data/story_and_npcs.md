# A Goblin's Quest — Story & NPC Design

## Story Synopsis

### Act 1: An Unlikely Hero (Goblin Village)

Grub, a young maintenance goblin, is sweeping the village square when a messenger runs past bearing news: all warriors sent to find the Goblin King have failed to return. The village is in panic. Shaman Zog proclaims the King ventured beneath Blackfang Mountain for the Crown of Endless Riches. Chief Bork calls for volunteers — nobody steps forward. Grub's broom breaks from nervous fidgeting. The Chief, exasperated, jokingly says "Even Grub could do better than this lot." The tribe laughs. Grub, taking it literally, grabs a satchel and heads out. No one stops him.

### Act 2: Into the Wild (Mushroom Woods to Troll Marsh)

Grub ventures through the Mushroom Woods where he meets strange creatures, discovers ancient paths, and finds his first real challenges. He helps a troll, navigates the Watchtower, and crosses the Troll Marsh — each step building his confidence and collection of odd items.

### Act 3: The Mountain (Blackfang Mountain)

Deep in the mines and ruins of Blackfang Mountain, Grub faces haunted armour, solves ancient puzzles, and uncovers evidence that the King was indeed here — and that something darker lurks below.

### Act 4: The Truth (Forgotten Depths)

In the depths beneath the mountain, Grub finds the Lost King — not a heroic figure, but a frightened old goblin who got trapped after a cave-in, long separated from the Crown. The Crown is real, but guarded by an ancient guardian. Grub must decide: face the guardian, claim the Crown, and save his tribe's future.

### Ending

Grub defeats the guardian (not through combat, but cleverness — using collected items), frees the King, and returns to the village as an unexpected hero. The Crown of Endless Riches turns out to be a ceremonial artefact — valuable not for gold, but for what it represents: hope.

## NPC Roster (12 NPCs)

### 1. Chief Bork
- **Location:** Goblin Village — Chief's Hut
- **Role:** Quest giver, story progression
- **Personality:** Gruff but fair, tired of leadership, secretly proud of Grub
- **Appearance:** Large goblin with a wooden crown, ceremonial scars, one eye
- **Initial dialogue:** Explains the King's disappearance and the failed warriors
- **Progression:** Gives encouragement after each major region is cleared
- **Post-ending:** Promotes Grub to Royal Advisor

### 2. Shaman Zog
- **Location:** Goblin Village — Shaman Hut
- **Role:** Lore exposition, magical item identification
- **Personality:** Mysterious, speaks in riddles, knows more than he lets on
- **Appearance:** Bent old goblin, staff with glowing crystal, ritual paint
- **Initial dialogue:** Tells the legend of the Crown of Endless Riches
- **Progression:** Identifies ancient relics, gives cryptic hints about the next region
- **Gives item:** Crystal Lens (can examine ancient inscriptions)

### 3. Grub's Mother — Marna
- **Location:** Goblin Village — Grub's Home
- **Role:** Emotional support, starter item
- **Personality:** Worried but supportive, packs Grub extra supplies
- **Appearance:** Warm-looking goblin with an apron, grey-streaked hair
- **Initial dialogue:** "Oh, my little Grub... you're really going, aren't you?"
- **Gives item:** Honey Cake (restores 2 hearts, first food item)

### 4. Merchant Gorb
- **Location:** Goblin Village — Market Stall
- **Role:** Item trading, hint merchant
- **Personality:** Cheerful, greedy, surprisingly helpful
- **Appearance:** Fat goblin with a merchant's hat, gold tooth smile
- **Initial dialogue:** "First customer of the day! Well, first customer this week..."
- **Trades:** Gold Teeth for useful items
- **Progression:** New stock appears after each region cleared

### 5. Professor Squig
- **Location:** Old Watchtower — Top Floor
- **Role:** Gadget maker, mechanical puzzle lock
- **Personality:** Absent-minded, overenthusiastic about machines, hard of hearing
- **Appearance:** Goggles on forehead, oil-stained tunic, spanner in pocket
- **Initial dialogue:** "WHAT? Oh, a visitor! Don't touch anything!"
- **Puzzle:** Needs Broken Gear to fix his lift machine
- **Reward:** Fixes the lift, granting access to the marsh

### 6. Bog Witch Mirelda
- **Location:** Troll Marsh — Witch's Hut
- **Role:** Magical item creation
- **Personality:** Sarcastic, lonely, secretly kind
- **Appearance:** Hunched goblin-witch with a pointed hat, warts, patchy cloak
- **Initial dialogue:** "Another adventurer. Let me guess — you need something."
- **Puzzle:** Wants a Giant Mushroom to brew a potion
- **Reward:** Gives Torch (needed for Dark Cave puzzle)

### 7. Troll Grum
- **Location:** Mushroom Woods — Broken Bridge
- **Role:** Obstacle NPC, puzzle gate
- **Personality:** Lazy, not very bright, loves mushrooms
- **Appearance:** Big grey-green troll with a simple tunic, constantly snacking
- **Initial dialogue:** "No cross bridge. Grum hungry. Grum sad."
- **Puzzle:** Give Giant Mushroom → Grum moves, reveals hidden key
- **Result:** Player gets Ancient Key to open the Watchtower gate

### 8. The Lost King — King Thorne
- **Location:** Forgotten Depths — King's Chamber
- **Role:** Central story character, emotional payoff
- **Personality:** Broken, ashamed, but kind beneath the despair
- **Appearance:** Ragged royal robes, bent crown, long matted beard
- **Initial dialogue:** "Another one? Tell my tribe I'm sorry. I failed them."
- **Progression:** Reveals the Crown is real and the guardian blocks the way

### 9. Guard Captain Grik
- **Location:** Goblin Village — Training Ground
- **Role:** Tutorial combat hint, blocks early exit
- **Personality:** Boastful, bitter that HE didn't get picked for the King's rescue
- **Appearance:** Armoured goblin, wooden sword, eye patch
- **Initial dialogue:** "Move aside, kid. This is warrior business."
- **Progression:** After Grub returns with the King, salutes him

### 10. Old Miner Moss
- **Location:** Blackfang Mountain — Mine Entrance
- **Role:** Area guide, hazard warning
- **Personality:** Grumpy, experienced, warming up to Grub
- **Appearance:** Dusty goblin with mining pick, lantern, hunched back
- **Initial dialogue:** "Go back, kid. Mountain's cursed. Or haunted. Or both."
- **Progression:** Tells Grub about the ancient ruins and the King's last known route
- **Gives info:** Password for the guarded mine door

### 11. Strange Hermit — Gromble
- **Location:** Mushroom Woods — Hidden Cave
- **Role:** Secret lore, joke character
- **Personality:** Speaks in non sequiturs, possibly insane, surprisingly wise
- **Appearance:** Wild-eyed goblin wearing a cooking pot as a helmet
- **Initial dialogue:** "THEY'RE IN THE WALLS! Oh. You're not a wall-person. Never mind."
- **Secret:** Reveals the location of a hidden Gold Tooth stash
- **Joke item:** Gives a "Magic Spoon" (does nothing, but has funny flavour text)

### 12. Goblin Child — Pip
- **Location:** Goblin Village — Behind the Blacksmith
- **Role:** Secret hint, adorable side character
- **Personality:** Mischievous, hero-worships Grub
- **Appearance:** Small goblin with a too-big hat, missing front tooth
- **Initial dialogue:** "Are you REALLY going to save the King?"
- **Secret:** Saw where the village's emergency Rope was hidden
- **Gives hint:** "The rope is behind the blacksmith's barrels!"

## Dialogue System Design

### Dialogue Node Format

```json
{
  "id": "chief_intro",
  "npc": "chief",
  "conditions": {},
  "nodes": [
    {"speaker": "chief", "text": "Ah, Grub. I suppose you've heard the news.", "next": 1},
    {"speaker": "chief", "text": "The King went missing beneath Blackfang Mountain. Three expeditions. None returned.", "next": 2},
    {"speaker": "chief", "text": "Look, I know you're just the maintenance goblin, but...", "next": 3},
    {"speaker": "chief", "text": "Actually, never mind. It's not your problem.", "next": null}
  ]
}
```

### Conditional Dialogue

NPCs respond differently based on game state:

```json
{
  "id": "chief_has_rope",
  "npc": "chief",
  "conditions": {"has_item": "rope"},
  "nodes": [
    {"speaker": "chief", "text": "You found the rope! The Woods entrance is that way.", "next": null}
  ]
}
```

### Multi-page / Branching

```json
{
  "id": "witch_torch",
  "npc": "mirelda",
  "conditions": {"has_item": "mushroom", "not_has_item": "torch"},
  "nodes": [
    {"speaker": "mirelda", "text": "Ah, a giant mushroom! I can use this.", "next": 1},
    {"speaker": "mirelda", "text": "Here, take this torch. The dark caves north of here won't light themselves.", "next": null, "give_item": "torch", "remove_item": "mushroom"}
  ]
}
```

## Story Progression Map

| Region | Required Items | NPCs Met | Puzzle Solved | Unlocks |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Goblin Village | Honey Cake, Rope | Chief, Marna, Gorb, Zog, Grik, Pip | None (tutorial) | Mushroom Woods entrance |
| Mushroom Woods | Rope, Torch (from witch) | Grum, Hermit | Broken Bridge (Rope + Plank) | Watchtower gate |
| Old Watchtower | Key (from Grum) | Squig | Broken Lift (Gear + Hammer) | Marsh entrance |
| Troll Marsh | Torch, Mushroom | Mirelda, Trolls | Dark Cave (Torch), Hungry Troll (Mushroom) | Mountain pass |
| Blackfang Mountain | Gear, Hammer, 3 Relics | Moss | Broken Lift, Locked Shrine | Forgotten Depths |
| Forgotten Depths | 3 Relics, Crystal Lens | King Thorne | Final guardian (item sequence) | Ending |
